Advantages of Urban Farming

The practice of urban agriculture does not limit itself to contributing in feeding the urban population, but rather provides important benefits at both the environmental and societal levels.



Preservation of biodiversity

Creation of resilient food systems

Reduction of greenhouses gases

Prevention of heat island effect

Wellness and performance in the workplace

Awareness to healthy eating habits
